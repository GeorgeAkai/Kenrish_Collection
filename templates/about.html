Our Story

Founded in 2017, Kenrish Collection has been at the forefront of beauty and style in Nakuru City. What started as a small beauty salon has grown into a comprehensive beauty destination.

Our passion for beauty and commitment to excellence has made us the go-to choice for clients seeking professional services and quality products.

Located in the heart of Shabab, we continue to serve our community with dedication, bringing out the best in every client.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional beauty services and premium products that enhance natural beauty and boost confidence in every client who walks through our doors.

We believe that beauty is not just about appearance, but about feeling confident, comfortable, and authentic in your own skin.

Our mission is to create a welcoming space where everyone can discover their unique beauty and express their personal style.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only premium products and maintain the highest standards
  • Professionalism: Our skilled team delivers expert services with care
  • Innovation: We stay current with the latest beauty trends and techniques
  • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction and comfort are our top priorities

Our Team

Our experienced team of beauty professionals brings years of expertise and passion to every service we provide.

From skilled hairdressers to beauty consultants, each team member is dedicated to helping you look and feel your absolute best.

We continuously invest in training and development to ensure we deliver the latest techniques and trends in beauty and style.
